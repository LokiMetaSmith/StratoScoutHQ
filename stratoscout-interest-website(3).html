Problem

County emergency managers miss opportunities to take preventative measures, costing lives and property, because they lack detailed and up-to-date geospatial intelligence at scale.

March 2025 Oklahoma Wildfire Outbreak: 4 dead. 2018 Rhea Fire: 290,000 acres burned. April 2009 Oklahoma Wildfire Outbreak: 140 homes lost.

Solution

We provide high-altitude, lightweight, affordable drones as a service to gather up-to-date geospatial intelligence at scale so emergency managers have the information they need to limit disaster impact.

  • Wildfires: dry vegetation, surface heat, and high-risk borders.
  • Flooding: location data to understand what is happening in specific areas.
  • Drought: maps and images that help determine critical next steps.
